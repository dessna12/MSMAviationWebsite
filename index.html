WHO WE ARE

MSM Aviation is a French company based in Lyon, France, operating in the helicopter industry


The driving principle of MSM Aviation is to be a reliable and transparent supplier of helicopter parts on all type of aircrafts at a fair price


WHY CHOSING US ?

Broad Helicopter services

  • Supply of helicopter parts for most aircraft
  • Supply of floatation and consumable
  • Repair services
  • Consulting in helicopter fleet

Technical knowledge

Our company has an exhaustive knowledge and experience in helicopter parts sales.
It is led by Sam Monnier, former flight instructor in the United States and Suriname and former Sales Director being in charge with Airbus business relationships.

Cultural diversity

At MSM Aviation, we are proud to adapt ourselves to cultural diversity. We have a worldwide clientbase and we can speak fluently 7 languages

Certified company
