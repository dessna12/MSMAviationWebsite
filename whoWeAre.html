MSM Aviation is a French company based in Lyon which operates in the helicopter market. Our primary objective is to be a reliable and transparent supplier of spare parts for all helicopter models with an international presence


Its CEO, Sam Monnier, pilot, flight instructor and former Sales Manager in a well-known European heliport, founded the company in February 2022 following its success in deploying a distribution network close to manufacturers and a trading service to international markets. His technical knowledge of parts is widely appreciated by its customers


MSM Aviation is a fast growing ISO 9001 certified company whose ambition is to become a transparent service platform at fair prices
